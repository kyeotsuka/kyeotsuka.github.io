Published on Aug 1, 2025 • 5 min read

Initial steps

Wireshark is an essential tool for any cyber security professional. Its ability to capture and display network packets in real-time is unparalleled. However, a busy network can generate thousands of packets per second, making it nearly impossible to find the needle in the haystack. That's where display filters come in.
